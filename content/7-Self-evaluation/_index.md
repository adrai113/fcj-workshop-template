## Areas for Improvement

While I have achieved certain milestones, I am well aware that there is still plenty of room for growth:
- **System Optimization:** Need to cultivate a mindset geared towards Cost Optimization and Performance, rather than just making things "work".
- **Technical English:** Need to improve my reading speed of AWS documentation and comprehension of English video lectures to optimize research time.
- **Advanced Troubleshooting:** Need to practice debugging complex systems, especially when combining multiple AWS services (e.g., VPC peering, advanced IAM Roles).
- **Automation/DevOps:** Need deeper hands-on practice with CI/CD pipelines and IaC (Infrastructure as Code) tools like Terraform or CloudFormation.

---

## Conclusion

The **First Cloud Journey** program has truly been an excellent launchpad. 12 weeks is not an exceptionally long time, but it was enough to clearly define my career path in **Cloud Infrastructure and Network Engineering**. The invaluable knowledge, skills, and connections gained from this program will undoubtedly serve as a solid foundation for my future tech career.
